Tara Kemp

Hi! I'm a PhD student in mathematics at the University of Queensland.

I am interested in combinatorics, particularly design theory. My current research is on latin squares and latin hypercubes, and my advisors are James Lefevre and Diane Donovan.

Email: t.kemp(at)uq.net.au
